WakiVo – Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how WakiVo collects, uses, and protects your information. By using our app, you agree to the terms outlined here.

1. Information We Collect

1.1. Microphone / Audio Recording (RECORD_AUDIO)

We access your device’s microphone only when necessary, including:

  • Sending a voice message
  • Recording audio during communication

We do not record audio in the background, and we never upload or store audio unless you intentionally send it.

1.2. Account & Device Information

We may collect limited technical information to improve app functionality:

  • Device identifier (for notifications)
  • App version
  • Diagnostic logs for performance improvements

We do not access personal data such as contacts, photos, or files.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your data is used solely to:

  • Send and receive voice messages
  • Deliver push notifications
  • Enhance app stability and performance
  • Maintain security and prevent misuse

We never sell user data under any circumstances.

3. Third-Party Services

We rely on trusted providers to deliver core features:

  • Firebase Cloud Messaging – push notifications
  • Supabase / Backend Services – message management and app operations

These services follow strict privacy and security standards.

4. User Data Protection

We implement multiple safeguards to protect your data:

  • Encrypted communication
  • Secure server infrastructure
  • No sharing of data with outside parties

Voice messages may temporarily pass through our servers only to deliver them to the intended recipient.

5. Children’s Privacy

The WakiVo app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Any updates will be posted on this page.
